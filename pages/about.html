How ICN Creator Works

This application allows you to customize your Investment Change Notice based on the various exception processing plans. It will incorporate all timelines, all exceptions, closed funds, and fund freezes.

First you will select the ICN type in the dropdown titled ICN type. Next you will select the effectice date and confirm if there are any additional exchanges. You will then be directed to download and complete the excel template for your mapping, fund adds, fund closures, and fund freezes. If a section doesn't apply with your plan then you can leave it blank. Once your file is complete, drop it into the dropbox and select Run.
